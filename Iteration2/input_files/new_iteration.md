- Decision: ITERATE
- Justification: The current project has reached a high level of maturity, but the identified methodological flaws—specifically the potential for data leakage via target encoding and the reliance on a potentially biased robustness threshold—threaten the scientific validity of the final candidate list. The "Publishability Delta" is high: by addressing the target encoding leakage (switching to one-hot) and performing the suggested sensitivity analysis on the robustness threshold, the manuscript moves from a "Borderline" study to a "Strong" contribution. The ROI is favorable because the infrastructure (LOCO-CV, SHAP, OOD analysis) is already built; the required changes are surgical rather than foundational. Furthermore, the hypothesis regarding Ni-based TMDs and electronic stability is scientifically compelling and warrants the refinement of the model to ensure the reported "robustness" is not an artifact of the current encoding or thresholding choices.
